Granola-Baked Oat Flan

Category: Breakfast | Snacks | Gluten-Free

Yield: 12 servings

Source: Sany

Ingredients

  • 500 g rolled oats (about 5 cups)
  • 150 g walnuts or mixed nuts (almonds, hazelnuts, sunflower seeds), roughly chopped
  • 4 tbsp honey (or more to taste)
  • 2 tbsp walnut oil (or melted butter or olive oil)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 8 eggs
  • 1 l almond milk (or cow's milk)
  • 1 banana, mashed (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C (340°F). Grease or line a large baking dish.
  2. Toast oats and chopped nuts with honey, walnut oil, salt, and cinnamon until lightly golden. Let cool slightly.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk eggs, milk, vanilla, and banana if using.
  4. Stir oat-nut mixture into custard mixture and let sit 5–10 minutes.
  5. Pour into baking dish and spread evenly. Bake for 40–50 minutes.
  6. Let cool at least 10 minutes before slicing. Serve warm or chilled.
  7. Store in fridge 4–5 days or freeze individual slices.
