Granola-Baked Oat Flan

Category: Breakfast | Snacks | Gluten-Free

Yield: 12 to 16 servings

Source: Sany

Ingredients

  • 500 g rolled oats (about 5 cups)
  • 150 g walnuts (or nut/seed mix), chopped
  • 4 tbsp honey (or more to taste)
  • 2 tbsp walnut oil (or butter or olive oil)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 8 eggs
  • 1 l almond milk (or cow's milk)
  • 1 banana, mashed (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C (340°F). Grease or line a large baking dish (23×33 cm).
  2. In a skillet or oven, toast oats and nuts with honey, oil, salt, and cinnamon for 5–10 minutes until golden. Cool slightly.
  3. Whisk eggs, milk, vanilla, and mashed banana in a large bowl.
  4. Stir in oat-nut mixture and let sit 5–10 minutes.
  5. Pour into baking dish and bake for 40–50 minutes, until set and golden.
  6. Cool at least 10 minutes before slicing. Serve warm or chilled with toppings.
  7. Store in fridge 4–5 days or freeze in slices for longer storage.
