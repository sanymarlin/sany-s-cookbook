Banana Almond Walnut Cake

Category: Snacks | Gluten-Free

Yield: 8 servings of 1

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe bananas – mashed
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups almond flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp cinnamon – optional
  • Pinch of salt
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts – plus extra for topping if desired
  • 1–2 tbsp honey or maple syrup – optional

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F).
  2. Grease or line a small cake pan (e.g. 8-inch/20 cm round or square).
  3. In a large bowl, mash bananas and mix in the eggs, vanilla, and honey.
  4. Add almond flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Stir until just combined.
  5. Fold in the chopped walnuts.
  6. Pour into the cake pan and smooth the top. Optionally, sprinkle with a few walnuts.
  7. Bake for 35–40 minutes, or until the top is golden and a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Let cool for 15–20 minutes before slicing — it will firm up as it cools.
