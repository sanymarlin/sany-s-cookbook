Tagliatelle with Chicken and Zucchini

Category : Main Dish

Yield : 4 servings

Source : Sany

Ingredients

  • 500 g tagliatelle
  • 4 chicken thighs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic - crushed
  • 3 zucchini
  • 15 g fresh basil - minced
  • 100 g grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Wash the zucchini, cut them in half lengthwise, then in half slices. Set aside.
  2. Sprinkle the chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the oil in a skillet. Sauté the chicken pieces on both sides until brown.
  4. Add the crushed garlic and sauté for 1 minute. The garlic should not become brown. Add the zucchini and mix. Cover and simmer until the chicken is cooked through, about 35 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, cook the tagliatelle according to package directions.
  6. Pour the chicken mixture over the hot pasta. Add the basil and the parmesan cheese. Mix and serve.
