Tagliatelle with Chicken and Zucchini

Category: Main Dish
Yield: 4 servings
Source: Sany
Ingredients:
500 g tagliatelle
4 chicken thighs
Salt
Pepper
4 tbsp olive oil
4 cloves garlic - crushed
3 zucchini
15 gr fresh basil - minced
100 g grated parmesan cheese

Instructions:

Wash the zucchini, cut them in half lengthwise, then in half slices. Set aside.
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper.
Heat the oil in a skillet. Sautée the chicken pieces on both sides until brown.
Add the crushed garlic and sautée for 1 minute. The garlic should not become brown. Add the zucchini and mix. Cover and simmer until chicken is cooked through, about 35 minutes.

Meanwhile, cook the tagliatelle according to package direction.

Pour the chicken mixture over the hot pasta. Add the basil and the parmesan cheese. Mix and serve.
