Joint of Roast Veal with Onions and Mushrooms

Category : Main Dish

Yield : 6 servings

Source : Granite Ware Roaster Pan Instructions

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg joint of veal
  • 2 sweet onions - sliced
  • 300 g button mushrooms - ceps or chanterelles, cleaned and sliced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 240°C (465°F).
  2. Prepare the Pan: Arrange the veal joint in a roasting pan. Coat it with the sliced sweet onions and mushrooms.
  3. Season: Sprinkle the veal and vegetables with salt and pepper.
  4. Roast: Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour, depending on the size of the joint. The meat should be browned, and the onions and mushrooms will become soft and flavorful.
  5. Rest and Serve: Let the veal rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve with the caramelized vegetables and any pan juices.
