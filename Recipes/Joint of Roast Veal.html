Joint of Roast Veal with Onions and Mushrooms

Category: Main Dish
Yield: 6 servings
Source: Granite Ware Roaster Pan Instructions
1.5 kg joint of veal
2 sweet onions - sliced
300 g button mushrooms - ceps or chanterelles -- cleaned and sliced
1 tsp salt
½ tsp black pepper

Preheat Oven:
Preheat your oven to 240°C (465°F).

Prepare the Pan:
Arrange the veal joint in a roasting pan.
Coat it with the sliced sweet onions and mushrooms.

Season:
Sprinkle the veal and vegetables with salt and pepper.

Roast:
Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour, depending on the size of the joint.
The meat should be browned, and the onions and mushrooms will become soft and flavorful.

Rest and Serve:
Let the veal rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
Serve with the caramelized vegetables and any pan juices.
