Creamy Lemon Herb Chicken

A creamy and delicious chicken dish with butternut squash, potatoes, and rice. Perfect for a hearty family dinner.

Category: Main Dish | Plat principal
Yield: 4 servings
Source: Sany & Image Generator Pro

Ingredients

  • 2 butternut squash - halved lengthwise and seeded
  • 8 potatoes - peeled and cubed
  • 4 chicken thighs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest (or zest of one lemon)
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp unsalted butter
  • 2 cups water or chicken bouillon
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup bell pepper - grilled and peeled
  • 500 g brown rice

Instructions

  1. Season chicken thighs with salt, pepper, paprika, oregano, and thyme. Dust lightly with flour.
  2. Parboil cubed potatoes for 5 minutes, then bake with the squash for 25 minutes until golden.
  3. Sear chicken thighs in olive oil and butter, then set aside.
  4. Create sauce by deglazing the skillet with water or bouillon. Add lemon zest, heavy cream, mustard, and Parmesan. Simmer chicken in the sauce with bell peppers for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Cook brown rice according to package instructions.
  6. Serve chicken, vegetables, and sauce over rice. Garnish with fresh thyme or parsley.
