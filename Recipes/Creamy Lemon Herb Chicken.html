Creamy Lemon Herb Chicken with Butternut Squash, Potatoes, and Rice

Category: Main Dish|Plat principal
Yield: 4 servings
Source: Sany & Image Generator Pro
2 pieces butternut squash, - halved lengthwise and seeded
8 potatoes - peeled and cubed
4 chicken thighs
salt
pepper
3 tbsp flour
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp paprika
1 tbsp lemon zest - (or the zest of one lemon)
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 tsp unsalted butter
2 cups water or chicken nouillon
2 cups heavy cream
1 tbsp Dijon mustard
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 cup bell pepper - grilled and peeled
500 gr brown rice

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients
Season Chicken Thighs:
Pat dry the chicken thighs and season them with salt, pepper, paprika, oregano, and thyme. Dust lightly with flour to help create a crust.

Step 3: Cook Vegetables
In a pot of salted boiling water, parboil the cubed potatoes for 5 minutes. Remove to a baking dish or cookie sheet.
Coat the potatoes and the cut side of the squash with oil. Sprinkle them with salt. Place the parboiled potatoes and butternut squash, cut side down, on a baking dish or cookie sheet. Cook for 25 minutes until lightly golden.

Step 2: Sear the Chicken
In a large skillet over medium heat, add the olive oil and butter. Heat heat until melted.
Place the chicken thighs skin-side down and sear for 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown. Remove from the skillet and set aside (they will finish cooking later).

Step 3: Create the Sauce
Add 1 tbsp of olive oil to the skillet. Add the lemon zest and sautée a few minutes. Pour 2 cups of water or chicken bouillon into the skillet, scraping the bottom to release the flavorful bits. Stir in the heavy cream, Dijon mustard, and Parmesan cheese. Mix until smooth.
Return the chicken thighs to the skillet, ensuring they’re submerged in the sauce. Add the grilled bell peppers. Simmer for 20-25 minutes, until the chicken is cooked and the sauce thickens.

Step 4: Prepare Rice
While the chicken and vegetables simmer, cook the brown rice according to package instructions. Season with salt to taste.

Step 5: Serve:
Serve the creamy chicken, vegetables, and sauce over the cooked brown rice. Garnish with fresh thyme or parsley if desired.

Storage: Refrigerate leftovers in an airtight container for up to 3 days.

Enjoy this hearty, creamy skillet dish that’s perfect for a family dinner!
