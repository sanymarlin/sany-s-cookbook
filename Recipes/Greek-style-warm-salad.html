Warm Salad with Roasted Potatoes, Green Beans, Feta, and Your Choice of Protein

Category: Salades | Main Dish
Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

500 gpotatoes – cut into bite-sized chunks
200 ggreen beans – trimmed
2shallots – peeled and thinly sliced
2 mediumtomatoes – diced
100 gfeta cheese – crumbled
50 golives – pitted and halved
200 gtuna – (or other protein) drained
2 tbspolive oil – for roasting
Salt and pepper – to taste
2 tbsplemon juice
1 tbspvinegar – (white wine or apple cider)
1 tspDijon mustard
4 tbspolive oil
½ tspsalt
¼ tspblack pepper

Instructions

Set your oven to 200°C (400°F).

Roast the vegetables: On a baking sheet, toss the potatoes and green beans with 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread in a single layer and roast for 30–35 minutes, stirring halfway through.

Prepare the protein: Tuna (drain), Eggs (boil, peel, quarter), Meat (cook and shred chicken or beef).

Make the vinaigrette: Whisk lemon juice, vinegar, and mustard. Slowly whisk in olive oil until emulsified. Season with salt and pepper.

Assemble: In a large bowl, combine roasted vegetables, shallots, tomatoes, olives, feta, and your choice of protein. Toss with vinaigrette.

Serve: Warm or at room temperature. Garnish with herbs if desired.
