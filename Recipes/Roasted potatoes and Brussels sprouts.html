Roasted Potatoes and Brussels Sprouts

Category: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
Source: Sany & and her AI Assistant
1 kg potatoes
500 g Brussels sprouts
3 tbsp olive oil
4 cloves garlic - minced
2-3 sprigs rosemary or thyme
1 ½ tsp salt
½ tsp black pepper
½ tsp paprika
10 gr Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

Preheat the Oven:
Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F) and position a rack in the center.

Prepare the Granite Ware Roaster Pan:
Lightly grease the bottom of the roaster pan with a teaspoon of olive oil to prevent sticking.

Prepare the Vegetables:
Wash and dry the potatoes and Brussels sprouts thoroughly.
Cut the potatoes into bite-sized chunks (approximately 2-3 cm pieces).
Trim the base of the Brussels sprouts and remove any yellow or damaged outer leaves. Slice in half lengthwise.

Season the Vegetables:
In a large mixing bowl, combine the potatoes and Brussels sprouts.
Drizzle with the olive oil, and toss to coat evenly.
Add the garlic, salt, pepper, rosemary or thyme, and paprika (if using). Toss again until evenly coated.

Arrange in the Roaster Pan:
Spread the seasoned vegetables in an even layer in the roaster pan.
Avoid overcrowding to ensure even roasting and browning.

Roast:
Place the roaster pan in the preheated oven.
Roast for 35-40 minutes, stirring halfway through, until the potatoes are golden and crispy on the edges and the Brussels sprouts are caramelized.

Optional Garnish:
Remove the pan from the oven and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese (if using).
Return to the oven for an additional 5 minutes to melt the cheese.

Serve:
Transfer the roasted vegetables to a serving platter or serve directly from the roaster pan. Enjoy while hot!
