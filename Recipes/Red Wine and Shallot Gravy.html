Red Wine and Shallot Gravy

Category: Sauces
Yield: 4 servings
Source: Sany and her AI Assistant
This sauce goes well with duck confit and roasted vegetables.
Ingredients:
2 shallots - finely chopped
1 clove garlic - minced
200 ml red wine
300 ml chicken stock
30 g butter
1 tbsp flour
2 sprigs thyme - or ½ tsp dried thyme
Salt - to taste
Pepper - to taste

Prepare the Base:
Heat 1 tablespoon of the duck fat from the confit in a pan.
Add the chopped shallots and garlic. Sauté until softened and lightly golden.

Deglaze with Red Wine:
Pour in the red wine and bring to a simmer.
Let it reduce by half to concentrate the flavors (about 5 minutes).

Thicken the Sauce:
Sprinkle in the flour and stir until combined with the shallot mixture.
Cook for 1-2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste.

Add Stock and Seasonings:
Gradually add the stock while whisking to avoid lumps.
Add the thyme, salt, and pepper.
Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

Finish with Butter:
Remove the thyme sprigs.
Stir in the butter for a glossy finish.

Strain (Optional):
For a smoother sauce, strain through a fine mesh sieve.
