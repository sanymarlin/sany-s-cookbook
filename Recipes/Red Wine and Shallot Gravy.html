Red Wine and Shallot Gravy

Category : Sauces

Yield : 4 servings

Source : Sany and her AI Assistant

Ingredients

  • 2 shallots - finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic - minced
  • 200 ml red wine
  • 300 ml chicken stock
  • 30 g butter
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 2 sprigs thyme - or ½ tsp dried thyme
  • Salt - to taste
  • Pepper - to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Base: Heat 1 tablespoon of the duck fat from the confit in a pan. Add the chopped shallots and garlic. Sauté until softened and lightly golden.
  2. Deglaze with Red Wine: Pour in the red wine and bring to a simmer. Let it reduce by half to concentrate the flavors (about 5 minutes).
  3. Thicken the Sauce: Sprinkle in the flour and stir until combined with the shallot mixture. Cook for 1-2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste.
  4. Add Stock and Seasonings: Gradually add the stock while whisking to avoid lumps. Add the thyme, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Finish with Butter: Remove the thyme sprigs. Stir in the butter for a glossy finish.
  6. Strain (Optional): For a smoother sauce, strain through a fine mesh sieve.
