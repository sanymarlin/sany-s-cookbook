Banana berries Snack

Category: Snacks

Yield: 20 servings

Source: Sany

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 500 g whole wheat flour
  • 200 g hazelnut flour
  • 400 ml almond milk
  • 50 g walnut oil
  • 4 bananas – mashed (≈ 400 g)
  • 30–40 g unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg (optional)
  • 150 g blueberries (or chopped strawberries or halved cherries)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease or line a large baking pan.
  2. Chop strawberries or cherries if used. Pat all fruits dry. Toss with 1 tsp flour to prevent sinking.
  3. Mash bananas in a large bowl. Add eggs, almond milk, and walnut oil. Whisk until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix whole wheat flour, hazelnut flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, salt, and nutmeg.
  5. Stir dry ingredients into the banana mixture until just combined.
  6. Gently fold in the prepared berries or cherries.
  7. Pour the batter into the pan and spread evenly. Bake for 35–40 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean or with moist crumbs.
  8. Cool 10 minutes in pan, then transfer to a wire rack. Slice into squares once fully cooled.
  9. Store in the fridge (up to 4 days) or freeze (up to 2 months).
