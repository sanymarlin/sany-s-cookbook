Fresh Banana Berry Snack Cake

Category: Snacks

Yield: 20 servings

Source: Sany

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 500 g whole wheat flour
  • 100 g almond flour
  • 120 g semolina
  • 400 ml almond milk
  • 50 g walnut oil
  • 4 bananas – mashed (≈ 400 g)
  • 30–40 g unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg (optional)
  • 150 g blueberries (or chopped strawberries or halved cherries)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease or line a large baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. If using strawberries or cherries, chop into small chunks. Pat all fruit dry and toss with 1 tsp flour.
  3. In a large bowl, mash the bananas. Add eggs, almond milk, and walnut oil. Whisk until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix whole wheat flour, almond flour, semolina, cocoa powder, baking powder, salt, and nutmeg.
  5. Combine the dry ingredients with the banana mixture. Stir just until combined.
  6. Fold in the prepared fruit gently.
  7. Pour the batter into the pan and spread evenly. Bake for 35–40 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean or with moist crumbs.
  8. Cool for 10 minutes in the pan. Transfer to a wire rack. Slice once fully cooled.
  9. Store in an airtight container in the fridge (up to 4 days) or freeze (up to 2 months).
