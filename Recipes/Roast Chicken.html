Roast Chicken with Herbs de Provence

Category: Meat
Yield: 6 servings
Preparation time: 5 minutes
Source: Granite Ware Roaster Pan Instructions
1 whole chicken - (approximately 1.5 kg)
1 ½ tsp salt
½ tsp black pepper
1 tbsp herbes de Provence

Preheat Oven:
Preheat your oven to 240°C (465°F).

Prepare the Chicken:
Place the whole chicken in a roasting pan.
Sprinkle the chicken evenly with salt, pepper, and herbs de Provence.

Cook the Chicken:
Cover the pan with the lid.
Roast in the preheated oven for 1 hour, or until the chicken is golden-brown with crispy skin and the internal temperature reaches 75°C (165°F) at the thickest part of the meat.

Rest and Serve:
Let the chicken rest for 10 minutes before carving.
Serve with roasted vegetables or your favorite sides.
