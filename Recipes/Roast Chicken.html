Roast Chicken with Herbs de Provence

Category : Meat

Yield : 6 servings

Preparation time : 5 minutes

Source : Granite Ware Roaster Pan Instructions

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken - (approximately 1.5 kg)
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp herbes de Provence

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 240°C (465°F).
  2. Prepare the Chicken: Place the whole chicken in a roasting pan. Sprinkle the chicken evenly with salt, pepper, and herbs de Provence.
  3. Cook the Chicken: Cover the pan with the lid. Roast in the preheated oven for 1 hour, or until the chicken is golden-brown with crispy skin and the internal temperature reaches 75°C (165°F) at the thickest part of the meat.
  4. Rest and Serve: Let the chicken rest for 10 minutes before carving. Serve with roasted vegetables or your favorite sides.
