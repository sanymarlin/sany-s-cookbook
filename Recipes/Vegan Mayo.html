Vegan Mayo

Category : Vegan | Sauces

Yield : 16 servings

Ingredients

  • 6 tbsp aquafaba - cooking water from chickpeas or dry beans (or the water from a can of chickpeas)
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • ¾ cup oil - with a light taste, e.g., grapeseed oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp maple syrup - optional

Instructions

  1. Place all ingredients in a jar. Wait until the aquafaba has settled at the bottom of the jar, a few minutes.
  2. Keep the stick blender at the bottom of the jar and mix on high speed with a stick blender. At some point, you will notice that the mixture starts thickening. Keep the blender at the bottom of the jar and continue mixing.
  3. At some point, you will notice the mixing noise changing significantly. This noise indicates that the emulsification has started. Only then, you can start moving the blender up and down, not too fast. Continue mixing until you reach the top and the mayonnaise is well blended.
