Chicken and White Bean Skillet with Vegetables

Category: Main Dish|Plat principal
Yield: 4 servings
Source: Sany
250g Dry White Beans
2 Chicken breasts
3 Carrots
8 Mushrooms
2 tbsp Freash chives
3 Celery stalks
2 tbsp Olive Oil
Salt - To taste
Pepper - To taste
1 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp rosemary

Prepare the White Beans:
Rinse 250g of dry white beans and soak them overnight in plenty of water.
Drain and rinse the beans, then place them in a pot with fresh water. Bring to a boil, then simmer for about 1–1.5 hours until tender. Drain and set aside.

Prepare the Vegetables:
Peel and dice the carrots into small pieces.
Slice the mushrooms thinly.
Chop the celery into small chunks.
Finely chop the chives.

Cook the Chicken:
Cut the 400g of chicken breasts into bite-sized cubes.
Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat.
Add the chicken, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned and fully cooked (about 7-10 minutes). Remove from the skillet and set aside.

Cook the Vegetables:
In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of olive oil.
Sauté the carrots and celery for 5 minutes until slightly softened.
Add the mushrooms and cook for another 5 minutes until tender.

Combine:
Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables.
Stir in the cooked white beans.
Add ½ cup (125ml) of water or chicken broth for moisture if needed.
Season with salt, pepper, and optional thyme or rosemary.
Simmer for 5 minutes to allow flavors to meld.

Garnish and Serve:
Sprinkle the chopped chives over the dish before serving.
