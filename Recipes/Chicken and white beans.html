Chicken and White Beans Skillet with Vegetables

Category : Main Dish | Plat principal

Yield : 4 servings

Source : Sany

Ingredients

  • 250 g Dry White Beans
  • 2 Chicken breasts
  • 3 Carrots
  • 8 Mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp Fresh chives
  • 3 Celery stalks
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt - To taste
  • Pepper - To taste
  • 1 tsp Dried thyme
  • 1 tsp Rosemary

Instructions

  1. Prepare the White Beans: Rinse 250 g of dry white beans and soak them overnight in plenty of water. Drain and rinse the beans, then place them in a pot with fresh water. Bring to a boil, then simmer for about 1–1.5 hours until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Vegetables: Peel and dice the carrots into small pieces. Slice the mushrooms thinly. Chop the celery into small chunks. Finely chop the chives.
  3. Cook the Chicken: Cut the 400 g of chicken breasts into bite-sized cubes. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat. Add the chicken, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned and fully cooked (about 7-10 minutes). Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  4. Cook the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of olive oil. Sauté the carrots and celery for 5 minutes until slightly softened. Add the mushrooms and cook for another 5 minutes until tender.
  5. Combine: Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables. Stir in the cooked white beans. Add ½ cup (125 ml) of water or chicken broth for moisture if needed. Season with salt, pepper, and optional thyme or rosemary. Simmer for 5 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle the chopped chives over the dish before serving.
