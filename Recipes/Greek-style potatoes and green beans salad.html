Greek-style potatoes and green beans salad

Category: Salades|Main Dish
Yield: 4 servings
500 g potatoes - cut into bite-sized chunks
200 g green beans - trimmed
2 shallots - peeled and thinly sliced
2 medium tomatoes - diced
100 g feta cheese - crumbled
50 g olives - pitted and halved
200 g tuna - (or other protein) drained
2 tbsp olive oil - for roasting
Salt and pepper - to taste
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp vinegar - (white wine or apple cider)
1 tsp Dijon mustard
4 tbsp olive oil
½ tsp salt
¼ tsp black pepper


Set your oven to 200°C (400°F).

Prepare and roast the vegetables:
On a baking sheet, toss the potato chunks and green beans with 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper.
Spread in a single layer and roast for 30–35 minutes, stirring halfway through, until the potatoes are golden and the beans slightly crisped.

Prepare the protein:
- Tuna: drain.
- Eggs: boil, peel and cut into quarters.
- Meat: cook and shred chicken or beef.

Make the vinaigrette:
In a bowl or jar, whisk together the lemon juice, vinegar, and mustard.
Slowly whisk in the olive oil until emulsified.
Season with salt and pepper.

Assemble the salad:
In a large bowl, combine the roasted potatoes and green beans, shallots, diced tomatoes, olives, and feta.
Add your choice of protein.
Drizzle with the vinaigrette and toss gently to combine.

Serve:
Serve warm or at room temperature.
Optionally garnish with fresh herbs like parsley or basil.
