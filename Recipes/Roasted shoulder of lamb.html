Roasted Shoulder or Leg of Lamb with Potatoes and Onions

Category : Meat

Yield : 6 servings

Preparation time : 15 minutes

Source : Granite Ware Roaster Pan Instructions

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg leg of lamb
  • 3 cloves garlic - sliced into slivers
  • 800 g potatoes - diced into large chunks
  • 2 medium onions - shredded or thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp herbes de Provence
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 125 ml water - approximately half a glass

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 240°C (465°F).
  2. Prepare the Lamb: Use a sharp knife to make small slits in the lamb and insert slivers of garlic into the cuts. Place the lamb in a roasting pan.
  3. Add Vegetables: Arrange the diced potatoes and shredded onions around the lamb in the pan.
  4. Season: Sprinkle the lamb and vegetables with herbes de Provence, salt, and pepper. Drizzle with olive oil and toss the vegetables lightly to coat.
  5. Add Liquid: Pour the water into the bottom of the pan to prevent drying and add moisture.
  6. Roast the Lamb: Cover the pan with a lid and roast in the preheated oven for 1 hour, or until the lamb is golden and tender, and the potatoes are cooked through.
  7. Rest and Serve: Let the lamb rest for 10 minutes before carving. Serve with the roasted potatoes and onions.
