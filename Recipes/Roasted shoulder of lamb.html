Roasted Shoulder or Leg of Lamb with Potatoes and Onions

Category: Meat
Yield: 6 servings
Preparation time: 15 minutes
Source: Granite Ware Roaster Pan Instructions
1.5 kg leg of lamb
3 cloves garlic - sliced into slivers
800 g potatoes - diced into large chunks
2 medium onions - shredded or thinly sliced
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp herbes de Provence
1 ½ tsp salt
½ tsp black pepper
125 ml water - (approximately half a glass)

Preheat Oven:
Preheat your oven to 240°C (465°F).

Prepare the Lamb:
Use a sharp knife to make small slits in the lamb and insert slivers of garlic into the cuts.
Place the lamb in a roasting pan.

Add Vegetables:
Arrange the diced potatoes and shredded onions around the lamb in the pan.

Season:
Sprinkle the lamb and vegetables with herbs de Provence, salt, and pepper.
Drizzle with olive oil and toss the vegetables lightly to coat.

Add Liquid:
Pour the water into the bottom of the pan to prevent drying and add moisture.

Roast the Lamb:
Cover the pan with a lid and roast in the preheated oven for 1 hour, or until the lamb is golden and tender, and the potatoes are cooked through.

Rest and Serve:
Let the lamb rest for 10 minutes before carving.
Serve with the roasted potatoes and onions.
